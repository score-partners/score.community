Hi everyone, especially @srudinac @Inske @dhaval

As mentioned in today’s Partner teleconference, Bradford Council has commissioned some drone footage of several large road junctions in the Bradford area.

It is hoped that using machine learning, image recognition etc. that traffic movement statistics can be obtained.
Much of this work is currently done manually with several people on location making direct observations and counting.
There might be other data that can be derived, perhaps regarding ‘pinch points’ where there is more possibility of collision, or areas where there are frequent inappropriate maneuvers - dangerous driving and there’s a lot of that in Bradford!
