Virtual Bradford: Creating a virtual city for the future

An online event as part of the University of Bradford’s UNIfy Festival.
This is a 3D city model / digital twin project initiated by the Bradford Council SCORE team and will produce an open 3D city model of Bradford city centre.

Speakers /panel members include:

  • Jason Longhurst - Strategic Director, Bradford Council

  • Martin Brynskov - SCORE and OASC

  • Bradford Council’s SCORE team

  • University of Bradford - researchers from the School of Archaeological and Forensic Sciences
