evdoxia.kouraki

Hi all & Happy New Year!

Great to see most of you at the first partnership meeting last week!

During the partnership meeting, my colleague @emilia.pisani and I mentioned that we are planning a series of replication workshops to assist SCORE partners to set up the first replication steps. Our aim is to help SCORE partners to replicate their solutions in other SCORE cities.

Our plan is to have one workshop every month, starting from February 2021 until May 2021. In every workshop, we would like to focus on only one SCORE solution as a case study and with the help of replication guidelines try to replicate it in interested cities.

So, SCORE Solution Owners please respond below in case your solution is ready to be replicated in other cities and you need help! Then we can book a call and discuss the details!

Prel. available dates for workshops can be found below:

23th of February 23, 10:00-12:00
16 or 17th of March, time tbc **
20, 21 or 22th of April , time tbc **
18 or 19th of May, time tbc**

Looking forward to your responses!!

Best

We would like to share with you the invitation to the “SCORE Living Lab Methods Replication Workshop” co-organised with IMEC living labs from Ghent and City of Ghent which will take place on Thursday, April 1st at 10:00-14:00 CET.

The idea of this workshop is to get to know some living lab methods to assess your solution before and during development, in order for you to make a solution that’s valuable.

We will be focusing on the following SCORE Solutions:

  • QR Code Toolkit: using QR codes to follow or report city objects and set-up IoT sensor proof-of-concept
  • Signalen: making it easier for residents to make a report and helping the city prioritize their response
  • Crowd flow dashboard: increasing crowd related data availability across city department.

ALL SCORE partners are welcome to join this workshop to learn living lab methods that will help you assess your solution before and during development!

Dear all,

We would like to inform you that we are planning our next replication workshop together with District09 and City of Ghent on the 19th of May, 9:30-11:30 with focus on QR Code Toolkit!

Aim with this replication workshop is to set up the next replication of the QR Code Toolkit to SCORE cities.

To kickstart your replication of this solution, District09 offers to provide your team with a clean copy of the QR Toolkit. A fully working toolkit of your own, including admin interface where you can start generating working QR Codes for your city departments (like Ghent has been doing successfully to set up a variety of use cases so far for QR Codes). We want to test with you if this approach to replication helps SCORE development teams to learn about the features, test advantages for yourself, apply everything to your city’s challenges and convince your city units to use this solution.

Please check below the updated replicability assessment of QR Code toolkit and let us know if you are interested to replicate the solution & join the workshop.
