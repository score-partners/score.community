Goodbye SCORE

Mariechoetarp

Dear SCORE consortium,

As I am leaving Aarhus University and with that, SCORE, this coming Friday (22 June)this is a friendly goodbye to wish you all the best of luck with this project. I am so happy to have been able to contribute in the process of making the marvelous visuals for SCORE and send it out during my time here.

On a practical note - if there is anything in the realm of communication you think is important to get out very soon, now is your chance.

I’ve also enjoyed all the talks I’ve held with each partner as it has greatly helped in the process of creating both a coherent understanding of SCORE and to facilitate that into something visual and communicative, that I can pass on to the next communications person.

So long,
Marie Choe Tarp

So Long, and Thanks for All the Fish

So Long, and Thanks for All the Fish is the fourth book of the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy "trilogy" written by Douglas Adams. Its title is the message left by the dolphins when they departed Planet Earth just before it was demolished to make way for a hyperspace bypass, as described in The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy. The phrase has since been adopted by some science fiction fans as a humorous way to say "goodbye" and a song of the same name was featured in the 2005 film adaptation ...
