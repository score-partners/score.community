“My Europe” event in Gothenburg

evdoxia.kouraki

Hej!

Johanneberg Science Park will present SCORE during the “My Europe” event next week with topic
“Vad gör EU-projekt i samhället?” (How EU projects contribute in society?) at the City library in Gothenburg. A mini fair will follow where citizens of Gothenburg will be able to get information about EU projects in the city (19 projects in total).

Organizers are the City of Gothenburg, Göteborgsregionens kommunalförbund and Europe Direct of Gothenburg Region.

The 19 EU projects that will be presented are:

  • College Väst
  • Den visa organisationen
  • Effektbutiken
  • eGO
  • Expand
  • Fossil-free Energy Districts (FED)
  • Framtidsentreprenörerna Fyrbodal
  • GR SAMSYV
  • Göteborg Tillsammans
  • Hikikomori
  • Innovationsarena Idrott, teknik och hälsa
  • IRIS
  • Nordic Taste & Flavour Centre
  • Nya GRannar
  • One Stop Future Shop
  • SCORE
  • Stadslandet
  • Test Site Sweden-West
  • UngÖst

Follow https://www.facebook.com/onestopfutureshop for more information!
