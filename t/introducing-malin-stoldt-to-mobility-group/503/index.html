Introduce yourself! Random
Hey all, Most of us don’t know each other so I thought it would be nice to tell everyone a bit about ourselves. I am Boris van Hoytema and am working with Bax & Company and Amsterdam to provide help on the topics of Open Source, Agile and Open Collaboration. Feel free to ask me anything. I’m super excited about SCORE mainly because I feel that learning how to use, develop and maintain software together is super important. And you?

Malin - the mobility group is currently exploring how the cities can collaborate/learn from each other/codevelop solutions. This includes solutions from Gent - MoniTHOR, TMaaS, Hamburg - Geoportal, Gothenburg - Traffiken.nu, Amsterdam - Travel Time Tool, Drukte Radar, as well as Aarhus - Impact of events on mobility. You can see the minutes that the mobility group has been taking here.

The cities of Bergen ( @Are ) and Bradford ( @phezoj ) are also keenly exploring wether they can/want to implement any of the above.
