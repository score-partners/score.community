There is also an EU Open Source Observatory webinar in two weeks.

Following our engaging webinar on OSOR Country Intelligence Reports, we are pleased to invite you to an interactive webinar on 'Guidelines for Sustainable Open Source Software (OSS) Communities in the Public Sector ’, which will take place on 4 June 2020 between 10:00 – 12:30 CET .

The objectives of the webinar are two-fold. Firstly, we will present our most recent work on the Guidelines. Secondly, we will work together with you to discuss what lies at the heart of sustainability of public sector OSS communities and brainstorm how the Guidelines could be further improved.

You will also have the opportunity to hear the experiences of working with and within public sector OSS communities from two projects. Fritjof Knier will share the story of the Integreat platform in Germany and Nephtis Brandsma will present the participatory digital democracy project through CONSUL in Groningen, the Netherlands.

To register, simply click on this link and you will receive the connection details shortly after: https://ec.europa.eu/eusurvey/runner/OSORSustainabilityWebinar
