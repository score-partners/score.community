WEX Smart Cities Expo

Mariechoetarp

WEX Smart Cities Expo is a Wallonian conference centered around highlighting local Smart City initiatives. Over the course of a day, experts from the public, private and scientific sphere will be giving responses to questions brought forth by regional and municipal officials, e.g.:

  • What are the benefits of my area becoming a Smart City?

  • How do I get started?

  • What are the different types of financing available to the municipalities?

The event takes place in Marche-en-Fammene , Belgium, and the primary language will be French.
