Dear SCORE partners,

The IEL HACKATHON is a competition aiming to solve real-life intelligent energy challenges and develop solutions for a sustainable future of Europe.

@emilia.pisani received an invitation from the organisers in case we would like to cooperate on the hackathon by:

• Proposeing cases for the participants;
• Recommending potential experts to evaluate proposals;
• Sharing the info about the hackathon.

The starting point in preparing for a hackathon is identifying and evaluating cases / challenges for the participants. The participating teams will compete in 4 different tracks contributing to the sustainable energy transition - Heating & Cooling, Circular Economy, Green Data and Green Transport. Each hackathon track will present at least 3 challenges.

Would any of you be interested to help the organisers to identify 3 challenges for the Green Data track ?

Hi everyone!

I would like to share with you the invitation to the Intelligent Energy Lab Hackathon.

Taking place on September 21-23th, 2021, with a live broadcast from Vilnius, the Intelligent Energy Lab hackathon is a virtual meeting point for engineers, IT product developers, scientists, startups and researchers who care about sustainable energy.

By solving intelligent energy challenges in real-time, you also get an opportunity to expand your professional network and win an exclusive prize worth 200,000 EUR – a chance to settle in Vilnius City Innovation Industrial Park and start operations in a land plot with fully developed infrastructure.

Please, find more information and register on the official website: https://iel.lt/hackathon/en/
