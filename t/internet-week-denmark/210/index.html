Internet Week Denmark

Mariechoetarp

Internet Week Denmark (IWDK)is an annual event held in Aarhus with events comprising everything internet related, from Hackathons to start-up workshops to talks in internet crime (and OF COURSE, Smart Cites!)

IWDK also has a great podcast and posts interesting articles on their website. Check it out at: https://internetweekdenmark.com/
