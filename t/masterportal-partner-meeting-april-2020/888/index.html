first of all I would like to thank you on behalf of Tino Wunderlich and myself for the interest and participation during the working table today.
As advertised during the working table I would like to provide you with further information.
The source code of the Masterportal can be downloaded here: https://bitbucket.org/geowerkstatt-hamburg/masterportal/downloads/examples.zip
If you would like to see the Masterportal live and try it out for yourself:
http://www.geoportal-hamburg.de/geo-online/
Hint: On the bottom right of your screen you can switch between english and german on the fly. Keep in mind that the information provided by the different services will still be in german language.

If you want to contact Jens Nienaber directly who is responsible for the product management Masterportal you can write an E-Mail to jens.nienaber@dataport.de
In order to contact the LGV which is responsible for the product maintenance Masterportal you can write an E-Mail to: pmmasterportal@dataport.de

If you have ideas or proposals regarding the Masterportal feel free to contact us anytime.
We would be pleased if you could send us suggestions for Masterportal use cases or general ideas regarding the Masterportal.
