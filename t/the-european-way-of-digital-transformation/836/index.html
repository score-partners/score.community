The European way of digital transformation

Janne

Hi everyone,

In continuation of our scale-up presentation during the Bergen meeting I would like to share some very relevant updates with all of you. The scale-up declaration “Join, Boost, Sustain” was officially presented during the Oulu Boost event last week in Oulu, Finland, where Martin Brynskov was host of the day.

I will encourage you all to read the press release and the declaration and share it in your networks in order to help creating the European way of digital transformation in cities and communities - very much in line with the rationale behind SCORE.

It is now ready to be signed by as many mayors as possible before the official signing ceremony is taking place during the Cities Forum in Porto, Portugal 31 January.

You can see the press release here

And you can find the official website for the declaration here: www.living-in.eu, where it is also possible for mayors to sign.

Best,
Janne
