Meanwhile, I was at MozFest this weekend where I ran into the people from Samsung Open Source and Mozilla IoT that had a suitcase with sensors with them to demonstrate IoT. Their suitcase had light sensors, moisture sensors (in a plant), sound sensors and an air quality sensor, on ‘prototyping boards’ linked together with the Mozilla IoT gateway. This seemed like a good way to get going.

kseniia

Hello Kim,

Thank you for following up and apologies I have not yet - the suitcase is on my agenda, just wanted to leave all the fun for the holiday season really.

I suggest that I propose a setup here, and we all discuss/tweak. My preliminary sense is that several small IoT projects, possibly along with separate functioning sensors as in Mozilla’s, could demonstrate what IoT is on a micro level but also how it gains more power and meaning once parts are connected to work together. Having written this, I see it sounds metaphoric.

As for the specific deadlines, I am thinking to have a proposal ready in January and the actual thing built by the next partnership meeting in Aarhus. We are deciding on the exact dates for this meeting, but a preliminary schedule says April. With such timeline, we could plan, discuss, choose the components, build and even polish a bit before we set the suitcase free to travel and embrace further transformation.

Do you have other thoughts or an earlier date when it would be good to have it?
