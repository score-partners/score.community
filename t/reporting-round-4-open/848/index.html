Dear SCORE partnership,

We still need some more time to formalise the budget change and Interreg also needs time to approve it. To avoid further delays, we have decided together with the JS to launch Report 4 and submit the change request afterwards. Partners are far from overspending so there shouldn’t be any problem. The 4th reporting round has thus been opened on the 28th of January .

The 4th report is a full report, meaning reporting on Finance is mandatory . You will have to report on activities carried out and expenses incurred between the 1st of July 2019 to the 31st of December 2019.

***** Important : For those partners who did not claim expenses in the 3rd report, the eligibility period for this finance report is the whole 2019! The activity report is still only about the second half of last year. (Stad Gent, City of Bradford, University of Bradford and Digipolis Gent)
